## Purpose

Projman is a demand tracking and delivery governance tool designed for a small IT team. It helps managers and admins visualize work from ideation to deployment, manage skill-aware staffing, and enforce timely delivery through configurable notifications and audit-ready history. The primary value is solving how to route requests, allocate the right skills, and prevent overloading teammates while keeping stakeholders in the loop.

## Tech Stack

Laravel 12.0, PHP 8.4, livewire, flux, flux-pro
